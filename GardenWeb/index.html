Purpose

This website is designed to educate people about the animals that live in New Zealand gardens. You can learn to identify common birds, mammals, and insects, understand their behaviors and habitats, and discover ways to protect and care for them. The goal is to inspire curiosity, appreciation, and respect for the wildlife that shares our backyards.
