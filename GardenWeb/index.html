Purpose

This website is designed to educate people about the animals that live in New Zealand gardens. Learn to identify common birds, mammals, and insects, understand their behaviors and habitats, and discover ways to protect and care for them. The goal is to inspire curiosity, appreciation, and respect for the wildlife that shares our backyards.

About Us

Garden Dwellers NZ provides facts, photos, and helpful tips to learn about the animals around us. By exploring this website, you'll discover how to identify creatures, understand their habits, and support the wildlife in your own garden.
